About Us

Formaspace Studio is a multidisciplinary design studio delivering architecture, interiors, and visualization solutions. We focus on functional design, premium aesthetics, and modern digital workflows to deliver meaningful spaces.

Who We Are

Formaspace Studio is a digital-engineering and construction solutions company built on real project experience and advanced technology. Founded by Syed Sajid Kirmani and Nadeem, we combine industry-proven engineering methods with modern tools like BIM, LiDAR, and high-fidelity 3D visualization.

From multimillion-euro cleanrooms to complex structural builds, our mission is to deliver accuracy, clarity, and control across every stage of a project.

Nadeem brings deep expertise in BIM, Revit, digital modelling, and coordination. His work ensures accurate, clash-free, and fully integrated digital environments that save clients time, cost, and risk.

  • BIM Modelling & Coordination
    • Architectural, Structural & MEP BIM Models
    • NAVISworks clash detection

  • Technical Drafting & Engineering Support

    • Ensuring real-world precision for decision making
